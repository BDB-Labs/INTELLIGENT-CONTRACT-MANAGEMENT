Section 7.2 Payment Terms
Subcontractor shall be paid on a pay-if-paid basis and payment is conditioned upon receipt of payment from the owner.

Section 8.4 Delays
No damages for delay shall be allowed. The sole remedy for delay is an extension of time.

Section 9.1 Claims
Notice of claim must be provided within 7 calendar days after discovery of the condition.

Section 12.6 Termination
Owner may terminate for convenience.
