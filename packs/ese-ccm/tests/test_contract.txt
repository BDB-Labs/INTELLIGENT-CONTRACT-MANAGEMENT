ARTICLE 5 — PAYMENT TERMS

Section 5.3 Payment:
Payment is contingent upon receipt of funds from the agency. Owner shall have
no obligation to make payment to Contractor until Owner has received payment
from the awarding authority. Contractor waives any right to assert a claim for
interest, penalties, or damages arising from delayed payment due to funding
delays outside Owner's control.

Section 5.7 Retainage:
Owner shall withhold ten percent (10%) retainage from each progress payment.
Retainage shall not be reduced below five percent (5%) regardless of project
completion percentage unless expressly authorized in writing by the agency.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ARTICLE 8 — TIME AND DELAYS

Section 8.6 Delay:
Contractor shall not be entitled to compensation for delays, including delays
caused by Owner, agency, or third parties. Time extensions, if granted, shall
be the sole and exclusive remedy for any delay, impact, or disruption to the
work. Contractor expressly waives any claim for lost productivity, extended
general conditions, escalation, or loss of efficiency.

Section 8.9 Liquidated Damages:
Liquidated damages shall accrue at $5,000 per calendar day for each day beyond
the Contract Completion Date. Liquidated damages shall apply regardless of
cause, except Force Majeure events as defined herein.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ARTICLE 10 — INSURANCE

Section 10.1 Required Coverage:
Contractor must carry the following insurance at all times during performance:
  - Commercial General Liability: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $4,000,000 aggregate
  - Automobile Liability: $1,000,000 combined single limit
  - Workers Compensation: Statutory limits
  - Umbrella/Excess Liability: $10,000,000 per occurrence and aggregate

Contractor shall name the Owner, Agency, and all Consultants as additional
insureds for ongoing and completed operations on a primary and non-contributory
basis. Waiver of subrogation is required on all policies.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ARTICLE 12 — INDEMNIFICATION

Section 12.1 Broad Indemnity:
Contractor shall defend, indemnify, and hold harmless Owner, Agency, and their
officers, directors, employees, and agents from and against any and all claims,
damages, losses, and expenses, including attorneys' fees, arising out of or
resulting from performance of the Work, regardless of whether such claim is
caused in part by a party indemnified hereunder.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ARTICLE 15 — TERMINATION

Section 15.2 Termination for Convenience:
Owner may terminate this Contract for convenience at any time upon seven (7)
days written notice. Upon termination for convenience, Contractor shall be
entitled to payment for Work completed to the date of termination plus
reasonable demobilization costs. Contractor shall not be entitled to
anticipated profits on unperformed work or overhead on unperformed work.
