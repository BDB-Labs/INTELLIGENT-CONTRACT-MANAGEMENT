## 4. Ensemble Output Certainty Assessment

### `_assess_response_certainty()` in `ese/pipeline.py`
- Automatic scoring (0.0–1.0) for every role output
- Multi-factor analysis: confidence field, evidence gaps, uncertainty notes, low-confidence findings, hedging language detection
- Three-tier classification: HIGH (≥0.75), MEDIUM (≥0.50), LOW (<0.50)
- Warning system: logs warnings when certainty drops below thresholds
- Curtailment flag: marks responses that should be curtailed due to critically low certainty

### Universal Prompt Reinforcement
- All roles now receive mandatory certainty/evidence requirements appended to their prompts
- Instructs models to state confidence level, list evidence gaps, curtail unsupported responses, and surface uncertainty

### Self-Reflection Loop (Opt-In)
- `_self_reflect_role_output()` in `ese/pipeline.py`
- Model critiques its own output on completeness, evidence, specificity, structure, and certainty
- If score < threshold, regenerates with improvement suggestions
- Configurable via `reflection.enabled`, `max_rounds`, `min_score` in config
